RULES

MANDATORY


  • Youngest player gets to be the Actor first.
  • No words, no sounds. No pointing to objects.
  • Alternate actors until everyone has had a chance to be the Actor.
  • Have Fun!

EXTRAS

  • Actor has a set period of time to act
  • Designate a score and time keeper.
  • Actors may make sounds other than speaking or whistling.

Disclaimer
These rules are not mandatory, and can be added or removed.
Discuss with your crowd which rules you intend to use before you begin.
